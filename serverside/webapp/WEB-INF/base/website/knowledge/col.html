Collection Permissions

First, select a Collection from the homepage by clicking on its thumbnail. 

 

1. Collection's Settings

Once in the desired Collection, click the arrow next to the Collection name to access the various Settings options.

2. Collection's Users

You can make a Collection visible/editable to any specific Users/Roles/Groups you wish to by clicking on the Users sub-menu, then adding the desired User/Role/Group from the provided dropdown menus.
Shown below:

The category tree shown in the homepage of the application will automatically adapt its behavior to the permissions you choose.

3. Collection's Custom Permissions

From inside a Collection, click on Custom Permissions, as shown:

Now, click on any permission you wish to customize, select any User/Role/Group/Etc that you want to enable that function for (from the dropdown menu), then click Add. 
Example shown below:

 

4. Add Followers

Owners of a collection can invite other editors to their collections. Once a collection is selected, open the Followers menu, and add one of the DAM users to the list.  After being added, they will receive email notifications when new assets are uploaded, approved or rejected within the collection.
